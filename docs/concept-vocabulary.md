# Concept Vocabulary

The neutral concepts used to tag provisions in the derived layer. Each describes what a provision is *about*; the vocabulary is doctrine-agnostic. Tagging is derived, unofficial, and traceable to each authoritative text.

- **`customer_due_diligence`** — Identifying and verifying customers and their circumstances (CDD/KYC/EDD).
- **`beneficial_ownership`** — Identifying the natural persons who ultimately own or control a customer.
- **`risk_based_approach`** — Assessing and mitigating money-laundering and terrorism-financing risk proportionately.
- **`amlctf_program`** — The governance, policies, and controls a reporting entity must maintain.
- **`suspicious_matter_reporting`** — Reporting suspicions to the financial intelligence unit (SMR/SAR).
- **`threshold_transaction_reporting`** — Reporting cash or other transactions at or above a set value.
- **`funds_transfer_reporting`** — Reporting international/cross-border funds transfer instructions (IFTI).
- **`cash_couriers`** — Cross-border movement of physical currency and bearer-negotiable instruments — declaration/disclosure at the border (FATF R.32).
- **`record_keeping`** — Retaining prescribed records for prescribed periods.
- **`tipping_off`** — Restrictions on disclosing that a report has been or may be made (disclosure offences).
- **`designated_services`** — Activities that bring an entity into the regime, including the Tranche 2 sectors.
- **`enrolment_registration`** — The obligation to enrol or register with the regulator.
- **`correspondent_banking`** — Obligations specific to cross-border correspondent banking relationships.
- **`politically_exposed_persons`** — Heightened measures for higher-risk public-office holders (PEPs).
- **`virtual_assets`** — Obligations for virtual-asset / digital-currency services (VASP/DCE).
- **`sanctions_prohibitions`** — Prohibited dealings with designated persons/entities or controlled assets.
- **`sanctions_permits`** — Authorisations/permits allowing an otherwise-prohibited act (licences).
- **`asset_freezing`** — Freezing of, and the prohibition on dealing with, controlled assets.
- **`designation_criteria`** — The legal criteria for designating or listing a person or entity.
- **`un_and_autonomous_sanctions`** — The UN and autonomous sanctions regimes and their relationship.
- **`proliferation_financing`** — Countering the financing of proliferation of weapons of mass destruction (FATF R.7; UNSCR 1540).
- **`enforcement_and_offences`** — Consequences of contravention; civil penalties and offences.
- **`supervisory_architecture`** — FATF mutual evaluation; national regulators (AUSTRAC; DFAT) and their functions.
- **`scope_and_application`** — Who and what conduct the instrument reaches; extraterritorial application.
- **`definitions_and_interpretation`** — Defined terms and rules of interpretation.
