FATF cross-jurisdiction crosswalk derived · unofficial

The chain from root to implementation: each FATF Recommendation is shown with its UN treaty / Security Council basis (the international instrument it restates), then how seven jurisdictions — Australia, the United Kingdom, the United States, the European Union, Canada, Singapore and Switzerland — each address it. It references FATF Recommendation numbers and topics only (not the copyright-restricted FATF text), and is a navigation aid — not legal advice, not an assertion that a provision fully or adequately implements a Recommendation, and not a claim of legal equivalence between the UN instrument and any national provision. Every national provision is a curated tag tracing to an authoritative text hash.
