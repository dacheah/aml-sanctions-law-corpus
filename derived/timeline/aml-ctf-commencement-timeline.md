## Commencement schedule

### 2024-12-10 — Royal Assent
*Commences:* Sections 1–3 and formal provisions  
The Amendment Act 2024 (No. 110, 2024) receives assent.

### 2025-01-07 — 28th day after assent
*Commences:* Schedules 9 (Pts 1–3), 11, 12  
Examination and information-gathering powers and definitions (Sch 9); REPEAL of the Financial Transaction Reports Act 1988 (Sch 11); transitional provisions enabling the Transitional Rules 2026 (Sch 12).

### 2025-03-31 — Fixed date
*Commences:* Schedule 5, item 2  
A tipping-off-related consequential amendment.

### 2026-03-31 — Fixed date — main reform tranche
*Commences:* Schedules 1, 2, 3, 5 (item 1 & Pt 1 Div 2), 6 (Pt 1), 8, 10  
AML/CTF programs and business/reporting groups (Sch 1); customer due diligence reform (Sch 2); regulating additional high-risk services — real estate, dealers in precious metals & stones, and professional services, i.e. the Tranche 2 sectors, with enrolment opening (Sch 3); tipping-off offence and disclosure of AUSTRAC information to foreign countries/agencies (Sch 5); services relating to virtual assets (Sch 6 Pt 1); transfers of value and international value transfer services (Sch 8); exemptions (Sch 10).

### 2026-07-01 — Fixed date — Tranche 2 obligations
*Commences:* Schedules 4, 7 (plus Tranche 2 obligations)  
Legal professional privilege (Sch 4); definition of bearer negotiable instrument (Sch 7). Tranche 2 obligations for the newly-covered sectors take effect (the Transitional Rules 2026 frame designated-service timing by reference to the start of 1 July 2026).
